Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with us. We value your satisfaction and aim to provide a fair and transparent returns process. Please read the following policy carefully to understand your rights and responsibilities when requesting a return, exchange, or refund.

1. Eligibility for Returns

Items may be eligible for return within seven (7) calendar days from the date of delivery. To qualify for a return, the item must be unused, unworn, and in the same condition in which it was received. The item must also be returned in its original packaging, including any accessories, manuals, and documentation provided at the time of purchase.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are not eligible for return. These include, but are not limited to:

  • Items that have been used, damaged, or altered after delivery
  • Products without original packaging or missing components
  • Clearance or final sale items (unless defective)
  • Perishable or customized goods

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, customers must first contact our support team or use the designated return request feature on the platform. Once the request has been reviewed and approved, instructions will be provided on how and where to send the item. Unauthorized returns may not be accepted.

4. Refunds

Once a returned item has been received and inspected, we will notify you of the status of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed to the original method of payment within a reasonable timeframe. Please note that processing times may vary depending on your payment provider.

5. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective, damaged, or incorrect. If you require an exchange for the same item, please follow the return process and indicate your preference for a replacement.

6. Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received was defective, damaged, or incorrect. Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless otherwise required by applicable law.

7. Inspection and Approval

All returned items are subject to inspection upon arrival. We reserve the right to deny a return or refund if the item does not meet the conditions outlined in this policy.

8. Late or Missing Refunds

If you have not received a refund within the expected timeframe, please check with your bank or payment provider before contacting us. There may be processing delays outside of our control.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Return Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact our support team for further assistance.
